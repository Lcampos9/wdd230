Target Audience

The target audience of the sanmarcoscommerce.com web site is described below:

  • Who: Business owners of the city, unemployed people, people that are looking for education.
  • Age: 14 to 60.
  • Technology: People with laptops, smartphones and all the available devices.
  • Income: Income levels will typicaly be between $300 or less annually.
  • Motivation: Improve the economy of the city and provide useful information to all the people that want to progress economically.

Two Personas

Sofia

  • Ocupation: Unemployed
  • Demographics and Education: 24 years old, bachelors degree.
  • Goals and motivation for using the site: This person is looking for a local job
  • Social: Very interactive person, she likes to look for jobs in the internet, very friendly
  • Technology: She uses a smartphone and a laptop.

Daniel

  • Ocupation: Systems Engineering
  • Demographics and Education: 27 years old, bachelors degree and currently studying
  • Goals and motivation for using the site: He is looking of a new opportunity to create a business in his city, he is looking for partners or someone that can help him.
  • Social: Very sociable, he likes to create more business connections, he likes to travel and know more about the economy of other countries
  • Technology: Smartphones, laptop.

Five Scenarios

  • Where can I meet someone that can help my business grow?
  • Is there any local website with all business added so I can partner with them?
  • How Can I start a new business in my town?
